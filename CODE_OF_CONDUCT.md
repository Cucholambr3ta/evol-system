# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone. We pledge to act and
interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive,
and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to affected parties
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the system

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Violence, threats of violence or violent language
- Insults, put-downs, or degrading language
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, following
- Publishing private information without consent
- Conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated fairly. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

Behavior that is determined to not align with this Code of Conduct may result
in a warning, temporary or permanent exclusion from the community.
